How we met

Jono's perspective

So when we first met is a hotly debated topic. However, I will tell the story of the time I remember meeting Zan.

I, being heavily into volleyball, was told by a friend of mine (Giselle) that we want to induct new members into the cult. Giselle had set up a friendly game with a few of the teams, so that these new members could see what they were in for. Zan being one of the new members obviously decided to join and the rest is history. Well, actually that doesn't explain anything.

A group of us decided that we would go to Sun City. Zan had some connections to get a better price (thanks Lidia) and decided to nervously phone me to tell me "you knows nothing Jon-o". Planning this trip, the actual trip and the months that followed, sparked a connection that still hasn't unsparked.

At Sun City is where things started to blossom. We realised we shared the same sense of humour and went out of our way to try to make the other laugh. Before we even got back from Sun City we knew we had not spent enough time together and planned to go to a cocktail making class. Which would turn out to be our first date.

The rest is history now? Well, I think there is a lot more to it but it can't all be in the "How we met" section.

Zan's perspective

Jono and I argue about this topic often. We ran in the same circles for a couple years before we really knew each other. I have found videographic evidence of this fact, which Jono chooses to ignore. But, it is true that volleyball was the beginning of our new found friendship.

We were a group of friends that had fun weekday meetups, pub quizzes and group trips that formed our friendship. Our quick wit and humour started setting things in motion, but it was one simple act of kindness that changed it all for me:
The usual group were out on a restaurant patio having dinner, when a typical Jo'burg evening storm erupted. I was sitting closest to the edge getting rained on slightly, but was too engrossed in the conversation to be all too bothered, but without a sound, without bringing any attention to himself, Jono got up from his comfy spot and sat next to me on the edge, blocking the rain from hitting me. It was at this moment that I knew I always wanted him in my life - and not just as a friend.

A lot of long phone call conversations and a bit of sneaking behind everyone's back (we really Monica and Chandler'ed this sitch), we eventually went on our first proper date - a cocktail class, and we've truly been inseparable since (unless of course you count the 13 Thousand KMs of land while we survived long distance).
